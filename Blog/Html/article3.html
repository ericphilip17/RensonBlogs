How to make money with WordPress blogging


Launching your website online and making money out of it are two different things. The former can be real quick, but the latter will surely take time. This section seeks to enlighten you on a couple of easy tips you can use to monetize your WordPress blog.


• Write content on your site.


For a start, you need is a typing device and internet connection. According to NetCraft, an England-based internet service provider, there are over 1.5 billion websites on the World Wide Web. Guess what? All these websites need fresh content. That means content writing cuts across all niches. Some platforms not only pay their users for writing content, but also have ranking systems, and that can be a great means to market yourself.


• Affiliate Marketing


There are many affiliate programs available on all niches. Once you've joined one of them, you're able to recommend their products to your site visitors by having attached liked on your websites. Every time your visitors click to buy the product, you receive a commission. Before opting into either of the programs, you should figure out what your clients may be interested in. Through this, you're able to track a larger audience and hence, accumulate more returns in the end.


• Using Google AdSense.


This is also a marketing plan offered by Google. To get started, add to your website a Google script. The script installs ads to your website, and every time your visitors click an ad, you bag some commission. The ads are either on a Cost Per Click (CPCs) or Cost Per Impression (CPMs) plan. CPCs are paid for when a visitor clicks an ad while CPMs are paid for every review.


• Website flipping


At times, companies or individuals prefer to purchase already existing websites. In such a case, you can become a website reseller. Having skills to build and deploy WordPress websites makes you way more qualified. You can build a website, get traffic and sell it later. You may consider using platforms like Flippa for ease of auctioning.


• Reviews Writing


This is a very easy method to use. You just have to identify a product, use it for free and thereafter write your experience as a review. However, you should endeavor to review only those products that are related to your niche, so that your visitors can capture the attention of your visitors.


As can be seen, there are many ways in which you can turn your WordPress blog into a real income site.
